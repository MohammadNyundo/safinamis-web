Story 01

It Started With a Question

In September 2021, during the COVID curfew, a student and his mother arrived in a town they didn’t know. It was late. Bags were heavy. Every hotel gate they knocked on was fully occupied.

Standing under a dim streetlight, one thought kept repeating.

“What if accommodation could be booked before arrival?”

That night, they found temporary shelter at a local mosque. The night passed — but the problem stayed.

That question quietly became the foundation of Safina MIS.

Movement without certainty — a feeling many remember.

STORY 02

We Were Just
Trying to Help

During my four years at university, my friend’s uncle asked us to build a simple Room and Stock Management system for his business, Shalom.

At the time, we didn’t know we were building anything big. We were just trying to help.

Over time, the Room Management part grew. Every improvement followed one principle: solve real problems for real people.

What began as a small university project slowly became Safina MIS.

Building something useful, long before we knew it would matter.
